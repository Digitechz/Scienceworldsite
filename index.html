About Us

SCIENCE WORLD is a committee of science enthusiasts of SSGMCE, Shegaon. We believe in nurturing an environment that excites everyone to see science through the lens of curiosity, exploration and application. We unleash the authentic nature of science to everyone through various events, activities, riddles, memes, analogies and animations that reflect our vision, "Doing science rather than reading science." Our aim is to help everyone in experiencing the magic of science by implementing it in our daily life. Join us on our journey to experience the vast universe of science, to skyrocket your wisdom.
